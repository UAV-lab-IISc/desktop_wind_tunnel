📋 CSV FILE REQUIREMENTS:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your CSV file must contain these columns:

Essential (REQUIRED):
  • CL - Lift coefficient
  • CD - Drag coefficient
  • Elapsed_s - Time in seconds
  • Temperature_C - Temperature
  • Pressure_Pa - Pressure
  • AirSpeed_mps - Air speed
  • ReynoldsNumber - Reynolds number
  • Lift_g - Lift force in grams
  • Drag_g - Drag force in grams

Optional (can include but not used):
  • Timestamp - Measurement time
  • Density_kgm3 - Air density
  • DynamicPressure_Pa - Dynamic pressure
  • CLCD_Ratio - Pre-calculated L/D
  • Any other columns

The script ignores extra columns and focuses on the 9 essential ones.
